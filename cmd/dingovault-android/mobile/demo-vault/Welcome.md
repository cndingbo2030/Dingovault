# Welcome to the outliner

Dingovault treats each **bullet** in your Markdown as a **block**. Blocks can be nested, reordered, folded, and searched instantly.

## Try these gestures

- **Indent / outdent** — Place the caret on a line and press `Tab` or `Shift+Tab` (desktop), or use drag handles where shown.
- **New sibling** — Press `Enter` at the **end** of a line to add another bullet at the same level.
- **Command palette** — `Ctrl+K` (`Cmd+K` on macOS) to jump to any page or search blocks.
- **Fold** — Use the disclosure control beside a parent to hide its subtree without deleting anything.
- **Light / dark** — Toggle theme from the toolbar to match your desktop.

## Navigate this demo

Use the path field or palette to open `Features.md` and `Advanced.md`, or click a [[Features.md|wikilink]] like this one.
